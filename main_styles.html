What is a VPN?
A VPN hides your IP address by letting the network redirect it through a specially configured remote server run by a VPN host. This means that if you surf online with a VPN, the VPN server becomes the source of your data. When you send information online a vpn creates a tunnel that encrypts your information this way if someone gets a hold of your data they won't be able to read it. The tunnel also makes it harder to hack in but don't be fooled it is still possible, just much harder to do.
